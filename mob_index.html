POWERUP YOUR PUNK

Choose items between rounds to further power up your punk. The longer you play, the more havoc you can create for your opponent.

Pick items that increase stats. Some items even allow for added effects to your attacks, like allowing all bullets to explode!

BATTLE UNIQUE PUNKS FROM OTHER UNIVERSES

Play as your favorite punk in an ultimate arena, battling opponents each from their own universe.

Take EVAK-47 for example: a cyborg assassin from the Cyberpunk universe.

Play online with other challengers or with your friends in private lobbies.
